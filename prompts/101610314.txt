Disorder Name: Dissociative drug dependence including ketamine or PCP, early full remission
Disorder Code: 101610314
Code Range: Not specified
Class Kind: category

Standard Definition:
After a diagnosis of Dissociative drug dependence including ketamine and PCP, and often following a treatment episode or other intervention (including self-help intervention), the individual has been abstinent from dissociative drugs during a period lasting between 1 and 12 months.

Exclusions: Episode of harmful use of dissociative drugs including ketamine or PCP; Harmful pattern of use of dissociative drugs, including ketamine or PCP
