Disorder Name: Opioid dependence, early full remission
Disorder Code: 1023217081
Code Range: Not specified
Class Kind: category

Standard Definition:
After a diagnosis of opioid dependence, and often following a treatment episode or other intervention (including self-help intervention), the individual has been abstinent from opioid use during a period lasting between 1 and 12 months.

Exclusions: Episode of harmful use of opioids; Harmful pattern of use of opioids
