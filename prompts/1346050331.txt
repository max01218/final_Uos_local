Disorder Name: Sedative, hypnotic or anxiolytic dependence, current use
Disorder Code: 1346050331
Code Range: Not specified
Class Kind: category

Standard Definition:
Current Sedative, hypnotic or anxiolytic dependence with use of a sedative, hypnotic or anxiolytic drug within the past month.

Exclusions: Episode of harmful use of sedatives, hypnotics or anxiolytics; Harmful pattern of use of sedatives, hypnotics or anxiolytics
