Disorder Name: Synthetic cathinone dependence, sustained full remission
Disorder Code: 1359176422
Code Range: Not specified
Class Kind: category

Standard Definition:
After a diagnosis of synthetic cathinone dependence, and often following a treatment episode or other intervention (including self-intervention), the person has been abstinent from synthetic cathinone use for 12 months or longer.

Exclusions: Episode of harmful use of synthetic cathinones; Harmful pattern of use of synthetic cathinones
