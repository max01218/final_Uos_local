Disorder Name: Disorders due to substance use or addictive behaviours
Disorder Code: 1602669465
Code Range: 6C40-6C5Z
Class Kind: block

Standard Definition:
Disorders due to substance use and addictive behaviours are mental and behavioural disorders that develop as a result of the use of predominantly psychoactive substances, including medications, or specific repetitive rewarding and reinforcing behaviours.

Clinical Description and Diagnostic Guidelines:

Diagnostic Guidelines:

• Consider impact of symptoms on personal, family, social, and occupational functioning
• Evaluate symptom severity and duration
