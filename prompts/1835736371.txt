Disorder Name: Episode of harmful use of other specified psychoactive substance
Disorder Code: 1835736371
Code Range: Not specified
Class Kind: category

Standard Definition:
An episode of use of a specified psychoactive substance or medication that is not included in the other substance classes specifically identified under Disorders Due to Substance Use that has caused damage to a person’s physical or mental health or has resulted in behaviour leading to harm to the health of others. Harm to health of the individual occurs due to one or more of the following: (1) behaviour related to substance intoxication or psychoactive medication use; (2) direct or secondary toxic effects on body organs and systems; or (3) a harmful route of administration. Harm to health of others includes any form of physical harm, including trauma, or mental disorder that is directly attributable to behaviour related to substance intoxication or psychoactive medication use on the part of the person to whom the diagnosis of single episode of harmful use of other specified psychoactive substance applies. This diagnosis should not be made if the harm is attributed to a known pattern of use of the specified psychoactive substance.

Exclusions: Harmful pattern of use of other specified psychoactive substance; Other specified psychoactive substance dependence
